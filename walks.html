Trips I've been on

Perrys Lookdown to Acacia Flats

I went on an overnight camp in the blue mountians on my own from Perry's lookdown carpark to Acacia Flat's campground. On the NSW national parks website it says the walk is only 2km, so I was expecting a pretty cruisy walk (and because of this, like an idiot, I started at 4pm at the peak of winter) however it was anything but.

Vietnam and Thailand

My girlfriend and I on a trip to vietnam and thailand from late 2019 to early 2020 (thankfully just before covid hit). We were away for 6 weeks, with about 3 of those travelling from Ho Chi Minh to Hanoi, 1 in Chiang Mai, 1 on Koh Lanta and the remaing week bouncing around Krabi, Bangkok and Koh Samui.
